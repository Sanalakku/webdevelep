About kudumbashree

Varalakshmi kudumbashree is a meloor panchayath based kudumbashree unit andwas started in 2015.there are total
9 members in their group. every sunday they conduct meeting to collect their weekly collections and to discuss their
progress and missions.They also conducts and celebrates every festivals and events in their unit and are also engaged
in may social activities.These kudumbashree groups have yearly missions.They where also engaged in the farming
tailoring,catering and small scale bussines as the kudumbashree mission is to empower the women.
